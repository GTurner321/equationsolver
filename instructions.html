USER
COMMANDS

Commands:

*n
or 
xn
Multiply both sides by a number (also negatives, eg x-3)
*n/m
or 
xn/m
Multiply both sides by a fraction (eg *-1/4)
/n
Divides both sides by a number
lhs*()n/m
Multiply left bracket by n/m
(also 'rhs', 'x', 'br', integer and negative inputs, eg rhsxbr-4)
lhs/n,/n
Simplifies fraction on left side (also 'rhs')
a/b=c/d
Replaces any seen fraction with an equivalent fraction
(also 
n/m=p
and 
n=p/q
)

Additional commands when brackets are removed:

+n
, 
-n
, 
+n/m
, 
-n/m
Add or subtract a constant to both sides
+nx
, 
-nx
, 
+n/mx
, 
-n/mx
Add or subtract a term with x (eg -4/5x)
